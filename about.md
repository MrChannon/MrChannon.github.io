## Biography

Long Chen is a second-year Master student at [Networked Robotics and Systems Lab](http://nrs-lab.com/) ,  School of Mechanical Engineering and Automation in Harbin Institute of Technology Shenzhen, advised by [Prof. Hao-Yao Chen](http://faculty.hitsz.edu.cn/chenhaoyao). His major is Pattern Recognition and Intelligent System. He is very interested in robotics, artificial intelligence, control systems engineering.

Before that, He received the B. Eng degree in Automation (Information Engineering) from Automation College in [Northwestern Polytechnical University](http://www.nwpu.edu.cn/) in 2019.
